Archie's story

This is my dog, Archie. The biggest pain in the butt there is.

Archie is an eight year old Boston Terrier that was rescued from a shelter by us.

When he was brought to the shelter he was a stay, found on the streets of Oakland CA.

Archie was ill at the time we rescued him and was only skin and bones.

Today, seven years later, Archie is a healthy, happy memeber of the family.
